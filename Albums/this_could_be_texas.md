## This Could Be Texas
By English Teacher

Oof. I know this album deserves more credit than I will give it. 

As always the lyrics go over my head but I can sense that they mean something.

This album is unique. Mixing elements of psychedelic, punk and rock we get a fragile energetically angry result. You might not be up for it or it might just be your jam. I can't say I'm in the mood to listen to this album often, but I can see how other people could like this.

However, there is one song on this album that lead me back to the album again and again. "Nearly Daffodills" is a sudden change of sound with hints of nostalgia and warmth unseen for the songs heard prior. Honestly this is just a banger.

So yeah, good stuff but not for me right now. I can see myself coming back to this in a couple of years and absolutely loving it but for now I'll just take from it what I like. 

5/10 (SORRY!)

FAV SONGS: The worlds biggest paving slab, Not everybody gets to go to space, Nearly Daffodills
LESS SONGS: Mastermind Specialism, You blister My paint
