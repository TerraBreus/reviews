## Equus Asinus
By Men I Trust

Very chill and cute album beginning great with some very beautifully quiet and soothing songs. The first few times listening to this album felt eerie but wholesome. Like I was walking through a haunted house of a lovely person. The spookyness feels more "old and forgotten" rather than scary.

After the first three songs we come across "Frost Bite" which seems a little odd placed between "Bethlehem" and "Heavenly Flow". It's not as great of a song (also no drums) which takes away some of the excitement when trying to journey through the album.

Around the halfway I point I usually lose interest. "Purple Box" feels a little too normal, happy and hippie to match the eery spooky but cute vibe that the first part of the album seems to have.

So what can I say? I like the beginning and perhaps I'll get round to like the second half of the album at some point. But I can definitely see myself taking out some songs and customize the album to my own liking.

6/10
 FAV SONGS: I come with mud, All my Candles, Heavenly Flow
 LESS SONGS: Frost Bite, Purple Box
