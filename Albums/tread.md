# Tread (2021)
*By Ross From Friends*

Such an amazing album with such beautiful flow. Similar to **Headache**, Tread hits a spot of melancholy and hope. Without any sensible words said, I felt both (nostalgic) pain and happiness. ... *I'm not in the headspace to give this album proper credit and I also don't need to*...

I often lose attention to finish the album when nearing the end. The final songs are long and perhaps a bit watery, but when giving proper attention can guide into a wonderful trance of feelings and thoughts.

This is definitely an album to listen to on psychedelics!

8/10
