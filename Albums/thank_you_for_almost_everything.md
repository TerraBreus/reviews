## Thank you for almost Everything
*By Headache*

Honestly more of the same. Only this time the beats don't feel as Old-School and the storyline feels less coherent. It's probably just me and might be worth a couple more spins but I couldn't get into it with the attempts I gave it. 

*My Head Hurts but the Heart Knows the Truth* brings forth a very dark image with a somewhat positive spin to it. It creates a strong contrast between the power of pain and love. In this new album everything seems to be a little lighter which unfortunately seems to take away Headache's characteristic force. 

The Artificial Voice is the same here as well. In the first Album it felt refreshing to hear an artificial voice speaking spoken word (I know I know), but this time it felt a little repetitive. I believe Headache missed out on some creativity here.

3/10
