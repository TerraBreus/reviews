# sassy things
_by GENDEMA_

Strap in boys. This album takes you in a flow balancing both the drive as the zen needed to focus. 

I will say this is not something I would actively listen to and thereby doesn't match itself up to Against All Logic (some EDM I can both study to as listen to on its own). However for a study session, this is absolutely perfect. Apparently the genre is **Atmospheric Drum and Bass** and perhaps it's the genre that I like more than the album. Though I've seen myself repetively coming back to this album after surfing on the algorithm, so it might just be a good album :)

7/10
