## Professional Development Week (CODAM)

Intense bootcamp to boost your professional appearance and help launch a career in Tech. From 16-03-2026 to 20-03-2026.
### Notes and Summaries
Bites of information I wrote down and processed.


```
Call to Action.
What is it you want: I want to kickstart my career as a junior developer.
This gives readers/recruiters a reason to contact you, it is a "call to action".
```

```
Personal Branding.
The overlap of who you are and how people perceive you. Your public image.
This is what we want to emphasise when looking for a job.
They are parts that are indeed YOU (not made up) and parts that others can see (visible).

Example (not about me);
I am a social, compassionate and hard working software engineer with a growing passion for legacy migration.
```

```
The HEART Method.
A small anagram for a developed introduction touching relevant fields for recruiters.
H; How you introduce yourself.
E; Experience.
A; Advantage.
R; Reason.
T; Touch of Human.

```
