## Forgetting Sarah Marshall (2008)
**Directed by Nocholas Stoller**

More than six months have passed at the time of watching.

Silly, stupid, hideous but enjoyable like McDonalds. It doesn't scratch any real surfaces but can make your evening feel just a little lighter.

I wouldn't recommend this movie to anybody unless they just want an easy sleezy romantic love comedy.

2/10
