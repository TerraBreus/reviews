## Blake 7 Season 1 (1978)

Got this from the library on DVD hoping to revitalize my DVD player. 

Maaaaybe... if this were the only form of content I had for a whole year I would have been able to finish it. It is slow and the form of sci-fi acting feels strange.

However I can see how a lot of the prompts and editing techniques used made this a real cult-classic even today. But for me it just felt slow and boring.

2/10
