## Sentimental Value (2025)
*Directed by Joachim Trier*

Painful and at times a little confusing. I never understood why the father wanted his daughter to play the part of his mother. As the viewer you see a correlation between the two but her father I believe didn't know (unless her sister told him).

Hartwearming and calm is what I remember about it, yet it didn't speak to me too personally. 

Loved Renate Reinsve in **The Worst Person In The World** and she did a very strong job here aswell.

6/10 *Inclined to give this more but it has been so long ago since I actually watched the movie*
