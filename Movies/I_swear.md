# I swear (2025)
*Directed and Written by Kirk Jones*

A movie to be seen again for sure! This movie got me sobbing in tears for more than two hours and afterwards I felt a sense of calm I do not experience often. 

The acting is superb and the story told is heart-warming, subtle and brutal all at the same time. You laugh, you cry, you empathize... this movie has it all and with characters/actors displayed so kind that it is hard not to like them. 

About the cinematography alot of good things could be said too, but when watching this for the first time little of that stands out when put into contrast of the wonderful acting of Robert Aramayo.

All I knew about this movie at the start was that it had good reviews (4.5/5 on Cineville!)... now I know why

9/10
