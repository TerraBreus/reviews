# Fantastic Beast: The Crimes of GrindelWald (2018)

*Directed by David Yates*

Me and Duna watched this on a Harry Potter high but we were both quite dissappointed. The movie is such a chaotic mess. It felt like either a fever dream or as if I was high. Duna was in fact (a bit) stoned and she couldn't enjoy the movie either. Perhaps some of the plot makes more sense with its prequel fresh in mind. We both hadn't seen the first movie since eight years ago or so and as a result we were left with a chaotic and strange story that was not enjoyable and rather hard to follow.

CGI has also gone to the next level. While this is ofcourse rather normal with the development of technology, it feels like little is left to the eye of imagination. Everything feels fake and plastic and there is little actual 'magic' that can be felt when watching (or reading) the original Harry Potter franchise.

If we weren't watching it together, we probably wouldn't have finished it.

2/10 (one point for the chinese lion/dragon creature and the little snifflers)
